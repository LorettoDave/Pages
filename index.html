We only charge if we can fix your problem. Our labour rate is £40 per hour; most issues are resolved in an hour or two. If parts or supplies are needed, we will source these at the best price we can. There is a one-hour minimum for callouts, with no charge for travel time. Payment is due when work is completed; we accept cash or BACS transfer.
